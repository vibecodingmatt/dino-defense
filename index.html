🧬 Research Lab

This is where DNA gets spent. You have 0 DNA to level up your base health, starting cash, and every weapon — permanently, with no cap. Every kill drops DNA (more at higher difficulty). Aim to keep your weapon levels near the difficulty level you're playing: a few levels ahead and it's easy, a few behind and you'll be overrun.
